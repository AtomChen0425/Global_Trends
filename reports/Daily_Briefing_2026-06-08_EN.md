The article introduces UniSHARP, an advancement upon the SHARP photorealistic view synthesis method. The primary objective is to enable universal monocular rendering that accommodates a wide spectrum of camera systems, including standard perspective, wide-field-of-view, fisheye, and omnidirectional panoramic cameras. This approach allows for a consistent representation regardless of the input camera's intrinsic parameters.

This has broad implications for applications requiring consistent photorealistic rendering from varied monocular camera inputs.
