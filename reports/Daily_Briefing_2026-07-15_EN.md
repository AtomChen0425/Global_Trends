The implementation relies on traffic interception, primarily using `mitmproxy` for iOS applications, to capture authenticated requests. The captured data is then processed to extract stable authentication tokens, device identifiers, and session information. An AI model, specifically mentioned as Claude, analyzes the captured endpoints and generates a Python client.
